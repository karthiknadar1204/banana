The Olympic Games and Taylor Swift’s Eras Tour are causing sudden demand shocks in local economies, which can appear to boost inflation. However, UBS says these events may not actually increase the cost of living for locals. The Paris Olympics has boosted demand and prices in various industries, including hotels and airlines. Despite the surge in prices, French consumers aren’t likely to feel its pinch. The method for calculating consumer price changes might pick up the spiking costs in industries associated with tourism, providing a distorted impression.
